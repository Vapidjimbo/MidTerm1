About Us

Restaurant Staff Information

Owner

The owner grew up in Germany and immigrated to the USA while a teenager. He found his love for sausage and beer while living in Germany, and grew to also love hamburgers after immigrating here. His first business was a hot dog stand that he operated into his late 20's. Then he too out a loan from the bank a started Wiener Works. The rest is history!

The Staff

Many employees have worked at Wiener Works over the years. A few have been here since the beginning. We are approaching 20 years of being open, and want to give a shout out to Bill, Sarah, and Melissa! Bill was our first employee. Sarah and Melissa are sisters and joined us about 12 years ago. Thank you for all the hard work!
