Restaurant Menu

Regarding Our Menu

Our menu is ever changing. Of course, we have our tried and true dishes that we will always have with us. However, we encourage or chefs to experiment when they have time. When something magical is created we offer it to our customers, and if we get enough positive feedback, it becomes part of our menu.

Toppings

The price varies depending on toppings chosen: Mustard, onion, tomato, relish, cheese, cole slaw, kraut, chili, pickles, ranch, and chips are available.

Drinks

Drinks are also available: 16oz - 24oz or pitcher. We offer Coke, Root Beer, Sprite, Lemonade, Mr. Pibb, and Tea. Beer also available on a rotating tap.
